Health & Safety at ARAM' PIZZA PIZZERIA

Info about ARAM' PIZZA PIZZERIA’s Healthy Measures and Safety Protocols

At ARAM' PIZZA PIZZERIA, we take health very seriously… even if our cheese prefers to bask in the sunlight. Here, with full transparency and dedication, we share all the measures we take to ensure every pizza arrives hot, safe, and delicious.

Our Hygiene Commitments

  • Continuous training: our entire team receives monthly training in food handling, allergen control, and hygiene practices.
  • Health checks: daily check-ins at the beginning of each shift ensure every team member is in top condition.
  • Equipment and personal hygiene: strict handwashing routines, glove use, and constant disinfection at every workstation.
  • Systematic disinfection: deep cleaning of surfaces, utensils, and contact areas several times a day with certified products.
  • HACCP control: analysis of critical control points at every stage — from ingredient reception to final plating.
  • Audited suppliers: we work only with verified distributors and maintain full ingredient traceability.
  • Labeling and allergens: a clear menu with detailed allergen and healthy ingredient information.
  • Contactless orders: sealed packaging, safe deliveries, and no-contact options available.
  • Ventilation and clean air: constant air renewal and preventive filter maintenance.
  • Responsible waste management: proper separation, recycling, and daily waste control.
  • Internal audits: regular inspections and detailed documentation to ensure continuous improvement.

Little Touches from Our Kitchen

Our oven performs its own “stretching exercises” between batches to keep the heat perfectly balanced, and the team has its own handwashing playlist — 20 seconds of rhythm with a dash of Naples spirit. The result: pizzas as safe as they are delicious.

Honorary Recognition

We are proud to hold the title of “Golden Seal of Food Safety and Gastronomic Well-Being”, awarded by Dr. Giacomo Lefèvre — a French-Italian microbiologist from Marseille, known for his peculiar inspection method: examining dough texture under a laboratory magnifier. After visiting our kitchen, Dr. Lefèvre enthusiastically stated:

“Here, even the mozzarella looks like it passed a medical exam.”
