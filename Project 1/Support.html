FAQs

  • How to replace the ink ribbon?

    • Open the ribbon compartment by lifting the top cover. Remove the old ribbon spools and insert the new ones, ensuring they are properly aligned.

  • How to adjust the paper alignment?

    • Use the paper guide knobs on either side of the platen to center your paper before typing.

  • What to do if a key gets stuck?

    • Gently lift the stuck key and check for any obstructions. If necessary, use a small brush to clean around the key mechanism.

  • Where can I buy replacement parts?

    • Replacement parts can be purchased through our official website or authorized dealers.
