Remington Heritage Co. honors the legacy of mechanical type. We are a small studio dedicated to the restoration, reproduction, and manufacture of pieces inspired by the great classics of mechanical typography.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the art of mechanical writing through high-quality products that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design. We believe in the value of focused, distraction-free writing experiences in today's digital age.

Craftsmanship & Values

Every Remington Heritage Co. piece is created with respect for traditional mechanical processes. Our work emphasizes precision, durability, and timeless design, ensuring that each machine is not only functional, but meaningful.

We value patience, focus, and the physical connection between writer and machine. In an era of constant digital distraction, our products invite a slower, more deliberate creative rhythm.
